Taste of Home

Food is a connector of sorts. It builds bridges between people of the same, or different, backgrounds and unlocks memories of the past. As a student who is attending school a bit away (more than a thousand miles) from home, I frequently use food to relive childhood memories and to share a bit of who I am, where I came from, with those around me. In this project, I will share some foods that remind me of home :-)
